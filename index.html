About Us

A procurement partner that understands compliance.

DNB Trading & Enterprises Pvt. Ltd. is a Kathmandu-based trading and procurement company working with government offices, educational institutions and private enterprises across Nepal. We handle the full procurement lifecycle — specification review, supplier sourcing, bid preparation, compliance documentation and post-award delivery — so our clients receive equipment that matches the specification, on time and on budget.

Our sourcing network spans ICT hardware, audio-visual equipment, networking gear, industrial machinery, aviation spare parts and security systems, backed by working relationships with manufacturers and authorized distributors. Every engagement is approached the same way: understand the requirement precisely, verify it can be met honestly, and deliver without shortcuts.

Registered Nepali private limited company
Experience across Nepal's public e-procurement (Bolpatra) system
Direct manufacturer and distributor relationships

What guides our work

  • Accuracy first. We match specifications exactly rather than offering the closest available substitute without disclosure.
  • Documentation discipline. Compliance papers, certifications and warranties are prepared correctly the first time.
  • Long-term relationships. Most of our engagements come from repeat clients and referrals.
  • Local accountability. A Kathmandu office and team, reachable directly — not through layers of agents.
